## First post - an intro

Hi! Thanks for reading my website. This was the day when I started to write on GitHub. It's quite challenging to unlearn and relearn what I have known from more than 20 years ago. I studied for a diploma in Computer Science. Computers and the internet were my main interests, but as I got distracted easily, I ended up changing my interest to language and education. So I started working as a teacher in 2008. Honestly, it was fun and my communication and leadership skills have improved since then.

Since I moved to Sweden, my interest in technology has arisen. I created some content and courses for my yoga and meditation. Let's say that I have learned a lot with different operating systems, editing, and illustration software. Unable to cope with the stress of working in the school industry, I chose to learn technical writing and e-learning design. And started to write this blog to document my journey and of course to keep writing and writing. 

So here I am now, learning to write about technical writing and instructional design. 

Are you curious about what's to come?
